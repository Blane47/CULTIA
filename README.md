**CULTIA** is an AI-powered digital platform designed to address the growing loss of cultural knowledge among young people in Cameroon.

Cameroon has over **250 ethnic groups**, each with unique traditions, folklore, values, and history. Much of this knowledge has traditionally been preserved through **oral storytelling**, which is now declining due to urbanization, globalization, and Western-centered digital content.

CULTIA uses **modern software technologies and Artificial Intelligence** to digitize, preserve, and teach Cameroonian culture in an interactive and engaging way.
